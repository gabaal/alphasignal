Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 05, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AlphaSignal (alphasignal.digital). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our terminal. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our institutional intelligence services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase).
  • Payment Information: We do not store credit card details. All payments are processed securely by Stripe.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with the terminal, including signals viewed, alerts configured, and technical performance (via Sentry and Google Analytics).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the AlphaSignal terminal.
  • Process your subscription and payments.
  • Send you technical notices, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Deliver real-time alpha signals and AI market briefings tailored to your interests.
  • Analyze trends and usage to enhance user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform:

  • Supabase: For secure authentication and user database management.
  • Stripe: For payment processing and subscription billing.
  • Sentry: For monitoring application stability and debugging errors.
  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and user behavior.

5. Data Security

We implement 256-bit SSL encryption and follow industry best practices to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR or CCPA, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account settings within the terminal or contact us for assistance.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and analyze terminal usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the terminal may not function correctly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@alphasignal.digital
